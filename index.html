Hi, I'm Nate. I like hanging out with birds, coaching runners, and making computers do things. Welcome to my little corner of the web!

For money I'm a software developer. Outside of work I'm especially interested in web accessibility and archival. You can check out a list of my software projects or some of my code.

I spend a lot of my free time working at a parrot sanctuary. I maintain a wiki about birds in Minnesota. In the fall I'm an assistant cross country coach for a Twin Cities high school.

I try to write as often as I can on a few different topics.
